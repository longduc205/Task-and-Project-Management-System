## Ethics in Proposing

![Image](/Users/azure_awabi/Downloads/Task-and-Project-Management-System/markdown_docs/02 Project life cycle and conception_artifacts/image_000010_c0e1ddb1288ac3b007c3caaebb1c8b8af5865749706f1ed4b5e1dbe8f730fa2a.png)

The Trap: Intentionally bidding low to win, planning to hike prices later.

## The Standard:

- Realistic Costing: Do not underestimate.
- Capability Honesty: Do not claim skills the team lacks.
- Risk Transparency: Openly discuss potential pitfalls.

![Image](/Users/azure_awabi/Downloads/Task-and-Project-Management-System/markdown_docs/02 Project life cycle and conception_artifacts/image_000011_825948408c4e5f447991fbc2e54450c490560e7df842a553f0398e7eb7d06437.png)
